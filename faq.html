Frequently Asked Questions

How should I send my design for the product?

Do expect an acceptable range of deviation from design to printed goods. For most similar results, do set your files to CMYK.

What kind of order should I get for my first time?

As there might be a different expectation and understanding, we recommend ordering a sample first.

Can I ask for additional product proofing?

Make sure you submitted the correct order. You can request proofing, but it will add production time by 2 days.

What if the products were defective on arrival?

You can claim defective products within 14 days of receiving them. This includes: mismatch of quantity, variety, defect in printing, and defect in supporting items (clasp, ring, etc)
